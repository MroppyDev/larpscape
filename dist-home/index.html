        Welcome to Larpscape

        Cantorne was sung into being by the Choir of Five, and one wrong note slipped into the final cadence. Forty-two years into the Quiet Measure, that note is stirring again — and somebody has to go retune the world. A free, original browser MMO in the old style: one tab, no download, 24 skills, and an examine button on everything.
